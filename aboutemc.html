Our Story

From a young age, hard work wasn’t just something we did — it was how we were raised. Nick spent his time working alongside his dad, a floor coverer by trade, but the work didn’t stop there. From floors to fixing things around the house, Nick grew up learning how to tackle all kinds of projects.

Jesse was the same way — helping his dad with whatever needed done, whether it was building, repairing, or putting up a pole barn on their property.

Reece also learned early on by pitching in with his dad, painting, repairing, and handling different hands-on jobs.

Each of us grew up working with our hands, learning to problem-solve, and understanding the value of doing a job the right way. As the years went on, our skills grew sharper and our careers led us deeper into the construction industry. Even while working full-time jobs, we always found ourselves doing side work — fixing, building, and helping friends, family, and neighbors with their projects. No matter how busy life got, the three of us always talked about one thing: starting a business together. We didn’t know at first what that business would be, but we knew we wanted it built on the same values we were raised with — honesty, hard work, and craftsmanship. With the wide range of skills we had between us, a handyman and construction service was the perfect fit. That dream became EMC Services. What started as three friends with a shared vision is now a company built on trust, quality, and the determination to treat every project like it’s our own home. At EMC, we’re more than just a crew — we’re a family of friends who still believe in rolling up our sleeves, doing things the right way, and leaving behind work we can be proud of!
